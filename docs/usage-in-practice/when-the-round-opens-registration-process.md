# When the Round Opens: Registration Process

When a round enters the **OPEN** state:

1. Navigate to the AutoCap Dashboard
2. Select the active round from the round selector
3. In the Participants table, click **“Register Now”**
4. Connect your Filecoin wallet when prompted as Burn Address
5. In the registration modal:
   * Review the displayed registration fee
   * Enter your DataCap Recipient Address
   * Confirm the registration details
6. The system simulates the transaction to verify that it will succeed
7. Approve the transaction in your wallet
8. Wait for on-chain confirmation
9. Upon confirmation, registration is complete

After registration, the dashboard will begin tracking **eligible FIL burns** originating from your Participant Address for the duration of the round.
